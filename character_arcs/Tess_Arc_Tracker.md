### Chapter 23: Tess's Vengeance (Month 11)

**THE ACTION:** Tess hunts Webb. Confronts him masked and alone.

**What She Does:**
- Brutalizes him severely
- Beats him within inch of his life
- **LEAVES HIM ALIVE** (Canon critical)

**Why She Doesn't Kill:**
- Not mercy—wants him to live with fear
- Or: Couldn't cross that line despite rage
- Or: Killing would make her like them

**State After:**
- **Emotional:** Cold. Empty. Vengeance taken but no satisfaction.
- **Secret:** Team doesn't know she did this
- **Physical:** Minor injuries from confrontation
- **Knowledge:** Webb will recover. System will protect him again.

### Book 2 Arc Summary

**The Betrayal Arc:**
- Believed father was good → Found evidence → Confirmed complicity → Confronted → Severed
- Parallel to Ahdia's secrets: both hid truth, both eventually exposed

**The Loss Arc:**
- Leta targeted → Protected her → Lost her anyway
- Webb killed Isaiah → Exonerated → Killed Leta → Will be exonerated again
- System protects killers

**The Vengeance Arc:**
- Witnessed murder → Decided to act → Brutalized Webb → Left alive
- Team doesn't know. Secret carried into Book 3.

**What She Learned:**
- Family can betray you
- System protects itself, not people
- Evidence doesn't equal justice
- Vengeance doesn't heal grief

**Setup for Book 3:**
- Carrying secret of Webb attack
- Korede knows her identity—will partner with her
- Father relationship severed
- Leta's death defines her going forward
- Radicalized: no longer believes in reform
