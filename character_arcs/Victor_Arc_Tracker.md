- **Full Name:** Victor Hernandez
- **Codename:** Crimson Sable
- **Age:** 30s
- **Identity:** Hispanic community organizer
- **Day Job:** Director of community center/non-profit
- **Work:** Food drives, ESL classes, after-school programs

- Wife murdered by gang (carries grief/rage)

### Skills

- Parkour

- Negotiation / de-escalation
- Cultural broker (English/Spanish, bridges generations)
- Strategic campaign planning

### Living Space/Work
- Community center in poor neighborhood
- Desk with flyers, donation jar (~$6 in coins)
- Wedding photo: wife's face scratched out with black marker (deep gouges)
