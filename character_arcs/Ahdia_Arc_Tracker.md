## Book 4: Stalemate → DBT Introduction

### THE POWERS TRUTH (Critical Plot Point)

**What happens:** Ahdia tries to use her powers in The Between. Nothing happens—no time manipulation, no temporal awareness. From her perspective, they don't work. She tries anyway, out of desperation or habit, and each time: nothing.

**The truth:** They DO work in The Between. That's what the signal IS.

**The mechanics:**
- Her powers don't affect local spacetime in The Between (so she perceives failure)
- Instead, they bleed OUTWARD across dimensional barriers
- Every "failed" attempt creates fold echoes/temporal wake
- These ripples propagate through dimensions and reach Earth
- Mother FAERIS detects them as anomalous disturbances

**The irony:**
- She tries her powers, thinks they failed, gives up
- But each attempt WAS the signal
- She's been calling for help without knowing it
- The thing she thought wasn't working is what saves her

**What this means for the plot:**
- Earth detects the disturbances (Chapter 15)
- Ruth's team realizes someone is alive in The Between
- Jericho's expertise makes him central to interpretation
- They trace the "wake" to send their picoframe message back

**Thematic significance:**
- Trying matters even when you can't see results
- DBT theme: You can't always perceive the impact of your actions
- She never stopped fighting—she just couldn't see it working

**When does she learn this?** After rescue (Chapter 31+). The team explains they found her because of the disturbances. Every time she tried and "failed"—that was her saving herself.
