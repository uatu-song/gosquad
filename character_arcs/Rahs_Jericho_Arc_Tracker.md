# Rahs Jericho - Character Arc Tracker

**Role:** Hidden mastermind, Dr. Doom analog
**Arc Summary:** Trusted ally → Revealed mastermind → Exile king in The Between
**True Nature:** Egomaniac sociopath who has been orchestrating events since before Book 1

---

## Character Foundation

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Dr. Rahs Jericho
- **Position:** CADENS Chief Science Officer
- **Specialty:** Radio wave detection, trans-dimensional frequency interpretation
- **Public Persona:** Brilliant but marginalized scientist, warm mentor
- **True Nature:** Cosmic-scale mastermind, sociopath with god complex

### The Cosmic Radio Archaeology

**Jericho's Unique Ability:**
- Interprets ancient radio waves and light traveling through space
- Decodes patterns others dismiss as noise
- Reads signals millions/billions of years old
- "Trans-temporal linguistic communication" (academia rejected this)

**What He Learned From the Stars:**
- The Helminth exists and consumes everything
- The Conclave farms worlds as cattle
- Bellatrix was imprisoned, would eventually escape
- A Temporalist would emerge on Earth
- Mother FAERIS has undisclosed capabilities beyond her stated function

**Result:** Jericho knows more about the cosmic picture than anyone on Earth—and has been planning accordingly for decades.

## The Master Plan

### Phase 1: Position (Pre-Book 1)
- Join CADENS as Chief Science Officer
- Gain access to Mother FAERIS
- Study her true capabilities secretly
- Wait for Temporalist emergence
- Establish Eidolon control (mass manipulation tool)

### Phase 2: Facilitate (Books 1-3)
- Connect Kain to Bellatrix (the phone calls)
- Accelerate Bellatrix's arrival through manipulated events
- Use Eidolon to create chaos, gather data
- Position himself as indispensable to all sides
- Maintain perfect "helpful mentor" mask

### Phase 3: Harvest (Book 4)
- Play "inside man" for Go Squad after CADENS falls
- Serve Bellatrix while planning betrayal
- Ensure rift opens at Mother FAERIS's location
- Escape with Mother FAERIS in staged "death"

### Phase 4: Rule (Books 5+)
- Build empire in The Between ruins
- Use Mother FAERIS's true capabilities
- Ally with Eidolon (temporarily)
- Eliminate all rivals and equals
- Eventually return to Earth as conqueror

### Book 1: The Connector

**Public Role:**
- CADENS Chief Science Officer
- Kidnapping victim rescued from warehouse
- Grateful survivor

**Hidden Actions:**
- Made the phone calls connecting Kain to Bellatrix
- Staged his own kidnapping for alibi/cover
- Firas died trying to "save" him (devastating irony)
- Positioned assets for future moves

**The Phone Calls:**
- Bellatrix couldn't easily communicate from dimensional prison
- Jericho received her trans-dimensional signals
- Relayed her instructions to Kain—with his own modifications
- Kain thought he was chosen; Bellatrix thought she was in control
- Both were serving Jericho's plan

**Book 1 End State:**
- Mask intact
- Cover established
- Kain guided toward artifacts and power
- No one suspects

### Book 3: The Complicit Survivor

**Book 3 End State:**
- CADENS fallen to Bellatrix

- Falls into rift clutching Mother FAERIS's core

**Book 4 End State:**
