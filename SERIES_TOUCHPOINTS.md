## The Therapeutic Framework

### CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) - Books 1-4
**Core belief:** If I control outcomes perfectly, I'll be okay
**Pattern:** Identify problem ‚Üí Apply solution ‚Üí Control result
**Why it fails:** Reality is uncontrollable; perfectionism is unsustainable

### DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) - Books 5-8
**Core belief:** I can hold contradictions and still act
**Pattern:** Accept reality ‚Üí Tolerate distress ‚Üí Act from values (not outcomes)
**Why it succeeds:** Both/and thinking enables functioning despite chaos

## The New Earth Migration Arc (Books 7-8)

### The Physics Problem

**Why Deceleration is Impossible:**
- At near-lightspeed, relativistic mass increases dramatically
- Slowing requires energy equal to what accelerated you
- Conventional propulsion can't generate enough thrust
- You'd need to carry fuel to slow the fuel that slows you (exponential problem)
- Without solution: vessel overshoots target, drifts forever, everyone dies

**Real-World Proposals (All Insufficient):**
- Magnetic sails (too slow)
- Laser deceleration from destination (requires civilization already there)
- Antimatter (can't produce/store enough)
- Generation ships (don't solve deceleration, just delay the problem)

### Why Designed Singularities Solve It

**Temporal/Dimensional Manipulation:**
- Ahdia/Firas can affect spacetime itself, not just objects within it
- Create localized field where physics operates differently
- Essentially: make the universe "agree" the ship is slower
- Not thrust-based deceleration - reality manipulation

**The Cost:**
- Massive baseline burn (cellular degradation)
- Extended use over deceleration period (weeks? months?)
- Would kill one person alone
- Together, they share the load - survivable but devastating
- Diana's cosmic perspective helps stabilize the field
- Bellatrix's Type IV knowledge guides the process

### Thematic Resonance

**"You don't have to be fixed to be worthy":**
- They're literally broken (baseline critical) AND saving humanity
- Not fixed, never will be fixed, still worthy

**Both/And Thinking:**
- Designed for purpose AND choosing it freely
- Resentful of being used AND grateful to help
- Leaving home (grief) AND finding new one (hope)

**The Ultimate CBT‚ÜíDBT Payoff:**
- CBT: "If I control everything perfectly, I can save everyone"
- Reality: Can't control everything, can't save everyone
- DBT: "Accept reality AND act from values"
- They accept they were designed, accept Earth is lost, accept the cost

## TTRPG Session Planning

### How to Use Touchpoints

Each touchpoint represents a **major story beat** that should be developed through TTRPG sessions:

1. **Define the scene context** (location, characters, stakes)
2. **Identify the therapeutic lesson** (what CBT/DBT concept is demonstrated?)
3. **Roll for complications** (chaos system introduces unexpected elements)
4. **Generate beats** (5-7 story moments per touchpoint)
5. **Track consequences** (what changes as result of this touchpoint?)

### Dice System for Touchpoints

**Touchpoint Success Roll (d10):**
- 1-3: Touchpoint achieved with major complication
- 4-6: Touchpoint achieved with minor complication
- 7-9: Touchpoint achieved cleanly
- 10: Touchpoint achieved with unexpected bonus

**Chaos Injection (d6 per touchpoint):**
- 1: Character death/permanent loss
- 2: Betrayal or revelation
- 3: Rescue or unexpected ally
- 4: Power cost/baseline burn
- 5: Relationship shift
- 6: No chaos (rare)
