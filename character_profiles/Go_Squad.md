# The Go Squad

## Team Overview
- **Founded:** ~4 years before Book 1
- **Leader:** Firas Bacchus (Rogue Leader)
- **Base:** Parkour Academy (Firas's business)
- **Mission:** Protect Caledonia citizens from crime, especially police abuse
- **Rules:**
  1. Stay secret, stay hidden, never take credit
  2. No guns (Night Knight argues but obeys)
  3. Protect civilians above all else
  4. Maintain secret identities (even from each other until Ch 12)

## Team Name Origin
- Firas hates the name "Go Squad"
- Given by social media influencer who spotted them
- Rest of team loves it and adopted it
- Originally called themselves "The Traceurs" (Firas preferred this)

## Standard Equipment
- Black tactical gear (Lycra, body armor, utility belts)
- Infrared/night vision goggles
- Smoke grenades
- Ham radios (Firas insists on radio jargon)
- GoPro cameras (to record police misconduct)
- First aid supplies

## Team Dynamics

### Identity Reveals (Ch 12)
- Originally kept identities secret (Firas's rule)
- Recently started showing faces
- Full reveal happens when:
  1. Victor shares first (to support Tess)
  2. Leah follows
  3. Ben joins in (everyone already knew—obvious personal trainer)
  4. Ruth confirms

### Enhanced Abilities (Post-Warehouse)
- All experience unexplained power boost during police ambush
- Cannot replicate powers consciously
- Powers panic-based (Gloom Girl's theory)
- Actually enhanced by Ahdia's selective time manipulation
- Secret remains until later books

### Team Chemistry
- Victor + Leah have protective dynamic
- Ben is tactical skeptic
- Tess is emotional heart
- Ruth is medical/moral authority
- Firas is overprotective leader

## Notes
- All have trauma/pain they're processing through vigilantism
- All working class except Firas (small business owner)
- Team represents diverse Caledonia communities
- "Good people" revealed through environmental details, not exposition
