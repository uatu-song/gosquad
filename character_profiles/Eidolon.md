# Eidolon - Fear/Anger Amplification Entity

**Type:** Cosmic Entity (susceptible to technological manipulation)
**Status:** Active (Books 2+)
**Nature:** Supernatural phenomenon that amplifies fear and anger in populations
**Controller:** Rahs Jericho (hidden, revealed Books 4-5)

---

## Overview

Eidolon is a cosmic entity that amplifies fear and anger in target populations. Publicly presented as a naturally occurring trans-temporal phenomenon detected by CADENS scientist Rahs Jericho. Actually, Jericho's equipment doesn't just detect Eidolon—it triggers and directs it. The phenomenon is being used to orchestrate mass violence for Jericho's research and potentially for Titan Strategic's conspiracy objectives.

## Notes for Writers

### When Writing Eidolon Effects (Books 2-3)
- Describe as external pressure on emotions
- Characters feel fear/anger amplified beyond reason
- Observable: Peaceful people suddenly violent
- NOT mind control (amplifies existing emotions, doesn't create new ones)
- Leaves characters disturbed by their own reactions

### Planting Flags for Reveal
- 87% prediction accuracy mentioned repeatedly
- Timing too convenient
- Jericho's explanations slightly too pat
- Equipment active during riots (if anyone checks)
- Correlation between predictions and events absolute

### The Reveal (Books 4-5)
- Show recordings: Jericho activating equipment during "predicted" events
- Body count accounting: How many died in orchestrated riots
- Recontextualize: Every Chapter 1-2 riot was murder
- No redemption: Jericho shows zero remorse (it was all data)
