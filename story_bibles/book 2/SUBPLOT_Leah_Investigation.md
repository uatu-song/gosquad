### Handling Sexual Abuse Content

**What to Show:**
- Harassment happened (establish fact)
- Impact on Leah (trauma, anger, determination)
- Pattern across victims (systemic, not individual)
- System enabling abuse (NDAs, legal protection)

**What NOT to Show:**
- Graphic details of assault
- Gratuitous harassment dialogue
- Victim exploitation for drama
- Trauma porn

**Approach:**
- Survivor-centered (Leah's agency, victims' consent)
- Clinical when discussing pattern (evidence-based)
- Emotional when showing impact (human cost)
- Respectful of real survivors (this mirrors reality)

### Audio Recording Ethics

**Leah's Recording Without Consent:**
- Illegal in some jurisdictions (one-party vs two-party consent)
- Ethically justified? (Documenting abuse for protection)
- Strategic calculation (knew she might need proof)
- Both/and: Violation of privacy AND necessary protection
- Eidolon reframes: Focus on recording ethics, not harassment content

**Reader Response:**
- Some will see recording as wrong (privacy violation)
- Some will see recording as smart (protecting herself)
- Both/and: Can be uncomfortable with method AND support exposure
- Mirrors real debates about evidence gathering in abuse cases

**Based on Real Patterns:**
- Access Hollywood tape (leaked, didn't matter)
