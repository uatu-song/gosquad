## VICTOR'S RESISTANCE PRACTICE (Beats 221-260)

**The Counter-Strategy**

### Victor Shares Discovery
- Noticed: Fear/anger responses "too big" for situations (Eidolon detection)
- Developed practice with Leta: "Check if fear is proportional, channel appropriately"
- Started teaching at organizing meetings (mutual aid networks)
- Spreading through community (200-300 people know it)
- Some activists responding differently to Eidolon manipulation after practicing

### How Resistance Practice Works
1. **Notice:** Recognize fear/anger rising
2. **Check Facts:** Is threat real? Is response proportional?
3. **Acknowledge:** If amplified (Eidolon), recognize it, don't let it control
4. **Channel:** Direct emotion appropriately (fear→caution, anger→action)
5. **Not elimination:** About not being controlled, not removing emotion

### Team Questions
- Ben: "You're teaching people to fight Eidolon?"
- Victor: "Teaching recognition of manipulation. Can't fight what you don't see."
- Ruth: "Effectiveness rate?"
- Victor: "Maybe 60-70% for people who practice consistently. But spreading slowly."
- Leah: "How many people practicing now?"
- Victor: "Few hundred through networks. Growing, but not fast enough."

### The Insufficiency Problem
- Eidolon's influence: Citywide, possibly national
- Resistance practice helps individuals (significant for them)
- NOT sufficient for electoral scale (can't reach millions by November)
- Projection: Maybe 40-50% of activists by Month 8
- By election (Month 11): Maybe 60% practicing, still insufficient
- Reality: They might do everything right and STILL lose
