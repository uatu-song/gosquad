**Using The Window:**
- Go Squad reaches CADENS, extracts Bourn alive
- Bourn reveals: "Titan global coup, coordinating through fusion reactor, midnight deadline"
- New objective: Must reach fusion reactor (Southwest facility)
- Distance massive, time short (hours to midnight)
- Translocation needed (Ahdia must burn reserves)

**At The Reactor:**
- Midnight approaching, coordination signal about to execute
- Must disrupt quantum-entangled communication network
- Requires: Bourn's technical knowledge + Ahdia's time manipulation
- Method: Freeze reactor core while Bourn disrupts quantum field
- Ahdia knows this exceeds her remaining reserves

### The Choice & Cost

**Ahdia's Decision:**
- Freezing reactor core long enough = burns her past survival threshold
- Baseline already <10% (minutes from transcendence point)
- Can do safe disruption (might miss window, she survives) OR massive freeze (guaranteed stop, she doesn't survive)
- **Chooses massive freeze:** Stops coup, burns herself critically
- Emergency CADENS treatment stabilizes her (barely)
- **Survives but at extreme cost:** Baseline drops further, knows she's on final borrowed time

### The Bitter Victory

**What Gets Stopped:**
- Bourn extracted alive with evidence
- Titan global coordination disrupted (delayed, not eliminated)
- Coup prevented (28 positions remain empty for now)

- Go Squad survives

**What Remains:**
- Eidolon weakened but not gone (will return Books 3-4)

- **Kain wins election anyway** (despite all evidence, fear manipulation worked)
- Civilians traumatized by what they almost did
- Ahdia critically depleted, knows end approaching
- Power vacuums still exist, will regenerate

**Both/And:**
