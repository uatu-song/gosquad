# Chapter 20: Confrontation and Distance

**Timeframe:** 9 AM Saturday → Noon (3 hours)
**Locations:** Penthouse living room

## Summary

Saturday morning after Ruth and Ryu discovered Exile Island. Go Squad gathers at penthouse (without Ahdia, who's still sleeping). Ruth has already disclosed discovery. Team splits on reactions: Tess/Victor/Leah amused by absurdity, Ruth/Ben concerned about strategic problems (power vacuums, unsustainable scale). Ahdia enters halfway through to celebrity homecoming greeting, realizes they know. Tries to deflect ("was gonna show you after Season 1") but Ruth doesn't buy it. Confrontation succeeds but costs both emotionally - honesty necessary AND pushes them apart. Discussion turns to Kain: Ahdia reveals she DID exile him (Month 4) but he melted/dissolved (avatar death) and appeared on TV next day via clone body consciousness transfer. Team realizes Kain is functionally unkillable through conventional means - exile/assassination useless against someone who respawns. Reinforces need for institutional strategy (media leak, DOJ, public pressure) instead of individual targeting. Both/and complexity: Effective system AND ethically questionable AND creating chaos AND unsustainable. Chapter ends with truth spoken but everyone more isolated.
