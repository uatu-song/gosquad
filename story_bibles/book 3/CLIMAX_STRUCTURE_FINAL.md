## CHAPTER 51: AFTERMATH

### Diana + Rivets Confrontation

**Location:** Resistance HQ (private)
**Characters:** Diana, Rivets, eventually Ahdia

**The scene:**
> DIANA: [staring] "You're from Iteration 3."
>
> RIVETS: "How do you know that?"
>
> DIANA: "Because I'm the one who erased it."
>
> [Silence]
>
> RIVETS: "You... what?"
>
> DIANA: "I time traveled. Three times. To save this Earth.
>        Each time I had to erase another timeline.
>        Yours was the third."
>
> RIVETS: "You destroyed my entire WORLD?"
>
> DIANA: "Yes. And I'd do it again to save this one.
>        But I didn't know... I thought everyone just ceased.
>        I didn't know some would be displaced."
>
> RIVETS: "Well now you know. There are others like me out there.
>         Survivors from the worlds you erased."
>
> DIANA: "I'm sorry."
>
> RIVETS: "Sorry doesn't bring back my squadron. My world."
>
> [Beat]
>
> RIVETS: "But I understand. Desperate times. Impossible choices.
>         You did what you thought you had to."
>
> DIANA: "That doesn't make it right."
>
> RIVETS: "No. It doesn't. But it means I'm not the only one
>         who lost everything."
>
> [They look at each other - victim and perpetrator]
>
> RIVETS: "Don't let it be for nothing. My world died.
>         Make sure THIS one lives."

**Emotional resolution:**
- Not forgiveness, but understanding
- Shared burden of loss
- Rivets choosing forward over revenge
- Diana accepting responsibility
- Foundation for eventual peace
