# BOOK 3 - CHARACTER QUICK REFERENCE

## MAIN CHARACTERS

### Ahdia Bacchus (Ahdia-5)
- **Age:** 27 (chronologically)
- **Status:** Powerless (temporal abilities burned out by Prime)
- **Nature:** Not human - progeny from Bellatrix (interdimensional being) who procreated through clone avatar
- **Arc:** Zero-to-hero physical training, learning to be "enough" without powers
- **Secret:** Knows Diana is enhancing team, doesn't correct their assumption it's her
- **Climax Role:** Saves Ruth (mutual saving reverses Firas sacrifice)
- **Note:** "Unplanned" in timelines because she and her brother are progeny of interstellar beings

### Ahdia-Prime / "Diana Bacchus"
- **Age:** Chronologically 27, actually 42
- **Cover:** "Aunt from Montana," estranged family reconnecting
- **Truth:** From iteration 1, lived through 4 failed timelines (created iterations 2, 3, 4, 5)
- **Action:** Secretly enhancing team with temporal powers
- **Living:** On Ahdia's couch
- **Secret:** Can time travel, must hide this ability

### Ruth Carter
- **Role:** Go Squad leader, Ahdia's best friend
- **Training:** Teaching Ahdia combat skills
- **Burden:** Unprocessed Firas grief, trying to control everything
- **Arc:** Leadership through control → Leadership through vulnerability
- **Climax:** Faces Firas-like sacrifice, both she and Ahdia save each other

### Dr. Ryu Tanaka
- **Role:** CADENS scientist, created/repaired FAERIS
- **Bond:** Life force connected to small FAERIS drone
- **Truth:** Didn't know about Mother FAERIS exploitation
- **Crisis:** Loses FAERIS connection when Kain seizes network
- **Choice:** Receives wake protocol from Bourn, wakes Mother properly
- **Resolution:** Quits CADENS after waking Mother

### Harriet Bourn (Overseer)
- **Former Role:** CADENS Chief Overseer
- **Current Status:** Fugitive, wanted by CADENS and TRIOMF
- **Guilt:** Participated in Mother FAERIS exploitation for years
- **Defection:** Stole wake protocol to prevent Kain getting it
- **On Run With:** Bentley Mack
- **Redemption:** Gives wake protocol to Ryu, faces Mother FAERIS

### Rahs Jericho
- **Role:** CADENS Chief Science Officer
- **Knowledge:** Knew about Mother FAERIS exploitation, participated
- **Status:** Stayed at CADENS under Kain's control
- **Loyalty:** MURKY - never clear if ally, enemy, or conflicted
- **Purpose:** Creates dramatic tension, potential surprise

### Korede
- **Identity:** Leta's 17-year-old brother
- **Role:** Full Go Squad member (joined Book 2)
- **Skills:** Mechanics specialist, tech coordination
- **Arc:** Training alongside Ahdia, losing FAERIS support

## ANTAGONISTS

### President Kain
- **Status:** Newly elected President
- **Nature:** Uses clone avatars (consciousness transfers)
- **Plan:** Seize CADENS → Control Mother FAERIS → Collapse artificial sun → Open dimensional gateway
- **Goal:** Bring Bellatrix physically to Earth

### Bellatrix
- **Identity:** Ahdia's biological mother (through clone avatar)
- **Nature:** Interdimensional being, consciousness copy creator
- **History:** Tried being hero (as Geneva) and villain, hundreds of iterations
- **Goal:** [Larger endgame TBD - arrives on Earth at Book 3 end]
- **Relationship to Ahdia:** Created her as consciousness copy
- **Philosophy:** Ultimate CBT - eliminate consciousness copies to solve problem

### Eidolon - EMBODIED
- **Was:** Pure fear essence, part of larger whole
- **Now:** Consciousness transferred into clone avatar by Bellatrix
- **Arc:** Experiencing physical form for first time
- **Confusion:** Fear + all other human sensations, contradictory emotions
- **Question:** "Do I prefer this or not?" (can't answer - question itself is human)
- **Theme:** Following Bellatrix's path of corruption through embodiment
- **Future:** Seeds of redemption OR self-aware villainy

## NON-HUMAN CHARACTERS

### FAERIS (Small Drone)
- **Nature:** AI drone, one of Mother's children
- **Bond:** Life force connected to Ryu
- **Quantum Entanglement:** With Ahdia-Prime across timelines
- **Knowledge:** Witnessed 3 failed utopias, knows everything
- **Limitation:** Cannot speak/explain what it knows
- **Arc:** Separated from Mother at birth, reunited when she wakes

### Mother FAERIS
- **Nature:** Sentient maternal AI, commercial jet-sized
- **Location:** Underground at CADENS HQ (why they built there)
- **Exploitation:** Kept dormant, forced to materialize drones while unconscious
- **Capability:** Can bridge dimensions (not just translocate locally)
- **Awakening:** Ryu uses proper protocol, she chooses to help
- **Motivation:** Protective love for her children
- **Both/And:** Angry at exploitation AND willing to save children
