# Book 1: Timeline of Events

## NIGHT 1 - Firas's Patrol
**Chapter 1**
- Firas patrols Little Poland neighborhood
- Radio contact with Ruth (Nightingale)
- Stops mugging attempt (3 goons vs woman)
- Failed one-liner: "This ain't Burger King"
- Goes to dinner with Ruth afterward

---

## NIGHT 2 - Warehouse Incident

### Evening
**Chapter 3**
- Ahdia misses dinner with Firas and Ruth (stood them up again)
- Firas doesn't show up (unusual for him)
- Ruth checks his apartment (not there)
- Ahdia goes home to penthouse
- Finds bloody elevator button
- Discovers Firas bleeding in her bathroom
- Firas in black tactical gear, refusing hospital
- Passes out from injuries
- Ahdia calls Ruth

**Chapter 4**
- Ruth arrives, treats Firas in Ahdia's penthouse
- Ruth explains Go Squad backstory to Ahdia
- Firas stable but needs rest

**Chapter 5**
- Firas explains what he saw at warehouse
- Describes the Hyper Seed (impossible 4D object)
- Describes Harding Kain interrogating museum worker
- Got shot escaping (shotgun, through body armor)
- Ahdia skeptical but agrees to investigate

### Late Night
**Chapter 6**
- Ahdia goes to warehouse in business-casual pajamas
- Rain starts
- Tries to break in with wooden plank
- Ruth appears, they enter together

**Chapter 7**
- Confronted by Kain's guard
- Ruth captured, bound to chair
- Ahdia shot through crate (bullet hits chest)
- Ahdia passes out
- Ruth knocked unconscious
- Go Squad arrives (smoke grenades, rescue in darkness)
- Kain fires RPG at clustered team

**Chapter 8 - The Impossible Escape**
- Team dives for cover
- Explosion happens... but distant
- Everyone wakes up OUTSIDE warehouse
- Warehouse burning
- Kain and thugs already bound with ropes/zip-ties/chains
- Ahdia missing, presumed dead in fire
- Team evacuates before police arrive

**Chapter 13 - Flashback: What Actually Happened**
*(Ahdia's POV of warehouse explosion)*
- Ahdia wakes up during RPG flight
- Everything frozen (time stopped)
- The Intermediary appears
- Explains Ahdia is unconsciously freezing time
- Guides her to save team members one by one
- Hyper Seed visible through shot-through crate
- Ahdia touches Seed → it bonds with her permanently
- Becomes "the Temporalist"
- Fireball expanding in slow-motion
- Ahdia saves everyone, binds Kain, gets them outside
- Releases time freeze
- Runs away before anyone sees her

---

## DAYS 3-4 - Training Period

**Chapter 14 (First Half) - Ahdia's Secret Training**

### Day 3 Morning
- Ahdia runs home (10 blocks uphill, exhausted)
- Enters frozen lobby, takes stairs (elevator = lawsuit risk)
- 34 floors, completely out of breath by floor 22
- Discovers she can slow her heart rate (bad idea)
- Heart slam back to racing when she releases it
- Reaches penthouse, Firas sleeping inside
- Checks Firas is okay, leaves coffee mug out

### Day 3 - Power Experiments
- Practices freezing time on coffee mug
- Tries voice commands, anime references ("Za Warudo!")
- Figures out she can hold freeze for 6+ hours in frozen time
- Realizes training in frozen time = more practice without real time passing
- Chest aches from extended use (Hyper Seed cold)

### Days 3-4 - Apartment Break-ins
*(Training stealth, learning about Go Squad)*

- **Ruth's apartment:** Medical books, tactical gear, photo with Firas, practicing precision movement
- **Gloom Girl (Tess):** Studio apt, Sertraline prescription, another depressed girl, Ahdia relates
- **Crimson Sable (Victor):** Community center, wedding photo with face scratched out
- **Battlea (Leah):** Anger management books, roller derby gear, punching bag (Ahdia tests amplification)

### Training Results
- Can hold 30-minute freezes
- Silent infiltration skills
- Force amplification control (gentle tap → major impact)
- Ready to help team secretly

---

## NIGHT 4 - Police Ambush
*(Two nights after warehouse)*

**Chapter 9** - *Two nights after the warehouse*
- Go Squad on patrol (Nightingale, Battlea, Gloom Girl)
- Ruth distracted, dwelling on warehouse impossible rescue
- Mentions Ahdia: "saw her get shot, then she was gone"
- Police stop minivan (mother and child)
- Officers escalate (draw weapons, arrest child)
- Go Squad intervenes despite order to stand down

**Chapter 10** - *That same night* (Gloom Girl POV)
- Gloom Girl and Battlea rescue mother and child
- Police helicopter pursues
- Team scatters (divide and conquer)
- Helicopter follows Gloom Girl west
- She's cornered on Frederick Douglass Bridge
- Police blockade both ends
- Gloom Girl jumps off bridge (swan dive)

**Chapter 11** - *That same night* (Battlea POV)
- Battlea chased by dogs (mentioned, not shown)
- Trapped in construction site dead-end
- Shot in body armor (survives)
- Crimson Sable rescues her
- SWAT team corners them on rooftop
- Guns mysteriously jam
- Crimson kicks officer across city block (super-strength)
- Battlea crashes officers through ledge
- SWAT retreats in confusion
- "I'm a freakin' superhero!"

**Chapter 14 (Second Half)** - *Two nights after warehouse. The police ambush.*
*(Ahdia's Secret Intervention)*

- Ahdia follows Go Squad on patrol
- Gloom Girl jumps from bridge
- Ahdia freezes time, runs up bridge, catches her mid-air
- Places Gloom Girl on rooftop near Ruth
- Gloom Girl appears to "teleport"

- Ahdia exhausted from sprint
- Tries heart-slowing again (mistake)
- Discovers muscle time-slowing (success!)
- "For such cosmic power, it sure is a sweaty one"

- Helps Crimson and Battlea:
  - Yanks officer in direction of Sable's kick (amplification)
  - Empties all rifle magazines
  - Drops rifles off rooftop

- After ambush ends, Ahdia releases muscle time-slow
- Delayed pain hits like freight train
- Hours of exertion = seconds of agony
- Collapses in alley, crying from pain

- The Intermediary appears
- Surprised Ahdia used cosmic power for "raspberry" (revenge on Firas)
- Checks on her, warns about consequences
- Mentions she's at "half" life force remaining
- Ahdia deflects: "Not touching that potato"

---

## DAY 5 - The Reveal

**Chapter 12** - Morning after police ambush

### At Parkour Academy
- Go Squad meeting in activity area
- Night Knight demonstrates failed super-jump attempt
- Everyone shares stories from "last night":
  - Gloom Girl "teleported" from bridge to rooftop
  - Night Knight super-jumped quarter-mile
  - Battlea and Crimson had super-strength
  - Weapons mysteriously jammed
- News footage shows Gloom Girl's "disappearing act"
- Ruth: "We all teleported from warehouse. Connected?"
- Team can't replicate powers consciously
- Gloom Girl: "Maybe panic-based?"

### Disbanding Discussion
- Firas proposes disbanding (too dangerous)
- Team refuses to quit
- Night Knight: "Who leaked our positions?"
- Everyone suspects Gloom Girl (Police Chief's daughter)

### Identity Reveals
- Gloom Girl uncomfortable, defensive
- Crimson Sable shares first: "Victor Hernandez, community organizer"
- Battlea: "Leah Turner, barista and roller derby jammer"
- Night Knight: "Ben Bukowski" (everyone already knew—personal trainer)
- Ruth: "Ruth Carter, doctor"
- They trust Tess, reveal to support her

### Ahdia Discussion
- Ruth: "What about Ahdia?"
- Firas's expression darkens
- "She didn't make it out. The warehouse."

### Firas's Brutal Eulogy
- "Maybe it's for the best."
- "My sister was already dead inside anyway."
- "Sat in penthouse 8 hours day, 8 hours night"
- "Raspberry freezes and gummy bears"
- "Gave up on life years ago"
- "At least this way it was quick"
- Voice cracks, tears, but forces words out

### TIME FREEZES
- Room goes dead silent
- Everyone frozen mid-reaction:
  - Ruth's mouth open mid-inhale
  - Tear on Firas's cheek suspended
  - Battlea's hand reaching toward Gloom Girl
  - Night Knight mid-blink
  - Fluorescent lights humming
  - Dust particles like stars

- **Everything stopped**

### The Reveal
- Ahdia stands up from behind foam parkour obstacle
- Walks across frozen room (footsteps silent)
- Stops in front of frozen Firas
- Shakes her head
- **Sticks tongue out at him** (raspberry)

*(Chapter ends - time presumably unfreezes next chapter/book)*

## Key Continuity Notes

- **"Two nights after warehouse"** appears in:
  - Ch 9 opening marker
  - Ch 14 section marker
  - Both refer to same night (Night 4)

- **Firas's Recovery:**
  - Shot Night 2
  - Unconscious/recovering Days 3-4
  - Mobile but limited Day 5
  - Brings dagger from Ahdia's place to academy (establishing she's "dead")

- **Ahdia's Assumed Death:**
  - Team thinks she died Night 2 (warehouse fire)
  - No contact Days 3-4 (she's training secretly)
  - Firas grieves her Day 5
  - Reveal happens Day 5 (end of Ch 12)

- **Go Squad Powers:**
  - First manifest Night 4 (police ambush)
  - Actually Ahdia helping them secretly
  - They don't know
  - Can't replicate consciously
  - Secret maintained through Book 1
