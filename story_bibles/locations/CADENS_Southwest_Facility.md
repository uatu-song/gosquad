## Writing Notes

### When Describing Facility:
- Ground cosmic scale in human details
- Show both promise (Type I goal) AND harm (immediate suffering)
- Real science accurate (research fusion reactor design)
- Environmental impact visceral (not abstract statistics)
- Political framing vs. material reality tension

### Avoid:
- Cartoon villainy (real corruption is banal)
- Science-solves-everything (technology is tool, not solution)
- Sacrifice zones as inevitable (environmental racism is choice)
- Progress narrative without costs (both/and thinking required)

### Aim For:
- Both hope AND critique (fusion IS good, implementation IS bad)
- Real physics (educate reader accurately)
- Environmental justice grounded (sacrifice zones are real pattern)
- Political satire sharp but not simplistic
- Visceral human scale impact (not just abstract concepts)
