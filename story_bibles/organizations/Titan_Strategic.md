## Writing Notes

### When Revealing Titan Strategic:
- Let connections emerge gradually (mystery structure)
- "Aha" moment when team compares notes
- Satisfying convergence (all investigations matter)
- Scale appropriate (big enough to be serious, not so big it's unbelievable)

### Avoid:
- Cartoon villainy (keep operations realistic)
- Instant pattern recognition (earn the revelation)
- Simplistic solutions (systemic problems resist individual heroism)
- Making every problem trace back to Kain (some things are coincidence)

### Aim For:
- Realistic shell company structure (research real financial crime)
- Environmental justice grounded in real extraction economy patterns
- Information warfare tactics match actual bot network behavior
- Political protection feels plausible (not magic conspiracy)
- Both/and thinking: Criminal AND legal, corrupt AND functional
