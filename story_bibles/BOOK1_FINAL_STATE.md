# Book 1 Final State

**Purpose:** What happened, who knows what, and where everyone ended after Book 1. Used by compression scripts to provide prior knowledge context for Book 2+.

---

## Major Events Summary

### The Warehouse Incident (Ch 6-8)
- Go Squad investigates Kain's warehouse
- RPG fired at team; Ahdia instinctively freezes explosion
- Hyper Seed bonds with Ahdia during this event
- Team survives without knowing why
- CADENS surveillance catches everything

### The Docks Reveal (Ch 19)
- Ahdia at 18% cellular integrity, critically depleted
- Runs 4 miles to save Ruth from enhanced Tank cop
- Freezes time, defeats cop, collapses
- Ruth watches her work in frozen time—sees everything
- First person besides CADENS to know the truth

### The Singularity Collapse (Ch 29)
- Kain integrates with Tamois Heart, becomes kaiju
- Ahdia uses opposing temporal forces (red acceleration + blue deceleration)
- She begins dissolving into higher-dimensional space
- Briefly contacts Bellatrix in the "hallway with infinite doors"
- Firas helps channel power, then injects her with final treatment
- Team forms human chain to pull Ahdia back
- Firas is pulled into singularity—displaced, not dead
- His last words: "Oh hey. I knew you were in there somewhere."

## Character End States

### Ahdia Bacchus

**Physical:**
- 18-24 months projected lifespan (extended via CR-7 treatment)
- Requires ongoing treatments (initially every 3 days, then weekly)
- Hyper Seed dormant after singularity exposure but still integrated
- Nosebleeds and strain still occur with major power use

**Emotional:**
- Grieving Firas intensely—guilt that he died saving her
- Beginning to believe she has inherent worth beyond usefulness
- Found purpose through Go Squad
- Carries weight of secrets and mortality

**Situation:**
- Lives in penthouse with stolen CADENS mobile command center on roof
- Operates as tactical support, not field leader
- Declined official CADENS membership
- Uses their equipment independently

**Powers:**
- Conscious temporal manipulation (freeze, slow, selective bubbles)
- Range ~30-40 feet optimal
- Every use costs cellular integrity
- FAERIS drones learning to replicate at ~10% effectiveness

**What She Knows:**
- Full extent of her powers
- She's dying from cellular degradation
- CADENS exists and monitors her
- Firas is "gone" (believes dead, actually displaced)
- Briefly saw Bellatrix in higher dimensions

**What She Doesn't Know:**
- Firas will return (Book 7)
- Treatment prevents transcendence, not death
- Visibility meter / Helminth tardigrade system
- Full Conclave farming operation truth

### Ruth Carter

**Role:** Go Squad field leader, Ahdia's co-conspirator and best friend

**Situation:**
- Recruited by CADENS (finally gets resources her research deserves)
- Developed CR-7 Temporal Variant treatment that saved Ahdia
- Leads team in field while Ahdia provides tactical support

**What She Knows:**
- Ahdia has time powers
- Ahdia is dying (18-24 months with treatment)
- Ahdia secretly provided team's "powers" all along
- Full CADENS involvement and equipment
- Treatment mechanics (not the hidden transcendence truth)

**What She Doesn't Know:**
- Treatment prevents transcendence, not death
- Cosmic context (Conclave, Bellatrix, farming operation)
- Firas will return

**Relationship to Ahdia:**
- Close friendship built on mutual life-saving
- Co-conspirators in managing truth with team
- "You're worth saving. Not because you're useful. Because you're a person who deserves to live."

### Firas Bacchus

**Status:** DISPLACED INTO SINGULARITY. Will return Book 7.

**What Happened:**
- Helped Ahdia channel opposing temporal forces
- Took final autoinjector from her hand
- Injected her instead of himself
- Pulled into singularity while team saved Ahdia
- Last words: "Oh hey. I knew you were in there somewhere."

**What This Means:**
- Not dead—displaced into dimensional space
- Recovery/return is possible (Book 7)
- Ahdia doesn't know this—believes he's dead
- His sacrifice = her survival + her guilt
