## SCENE 1: The Aftermath

**Location:** The Between - panic room
**Characters:** Ahdia, AR-Ryu

**TTRPG Results:**
- Roll: 17 (Success)
- Approach: Opens up to AR-Ryu about what she saw

### STORY BEATS:

**Setup:**
1. Hours since the mass grave. Maybe a day. Ahdia hasn't slept.
2. Every time she closes her eyes: stasis pods. Children. Families frozen mid-flight.
3. The panic room feels smaller now. The walls she thought were shelter feel like a tomb.
4. AR-Ryu running standard checks: "Battery at 94%. Water reserves stable. Recommend sleep cycle."
5. She doesn't respond.

**The Spiral:**
6. The number won't leave her head. 72 hours.
7. Seventy-two hours from first breach to total population loss.
8. They had technology Earth can't dream of. Ships. Stasis. Infrastructure.
9. None of it mattered.
10. "Is this what's coming?" She doesn't realize she's said it out loud.
11. AR-Ryu: "Clarify query."
12. She looks at him. The visor. The blue glow. The only other presence in this entire dimension.

**Opening Up:**
13. She starts talking. Not to get answers. Just to not be alone with it.
14. "I found a graveyard today."
15. AR-Ryu waits. Doesn't interrupt with data.
16. "Hundreds of them. Maybe thousands. Families. Children."
17. Her voice cracks but she keeps going.
18. "They built all of this. These ruins. This whole place. They had centuries on us. Maybe millennia."
19. "And they died in three days."
20. Silence.
21. "What chance do we have? What chance does Earth have?"

**SUCCESS - She Let Him In:**
22. She's not asking him as a tool. She's asking him as... someone.
23. First time since The Between she's treated him like a person who might understand.
24. First time she's let anyone see her this raw.
25. The question hangs in the air between them.
26. And AR-Ryu has to decide how to answer.

### CONSEQUENCES SEEDED:

- Ahdia has opened up for the first time since arriving
- She's treating AR-Ryu as a person, not a utility
- The door is open for him to respond in kind
- Her fear is existential - not just for herself, but for Earth

### EMOTIONAL ARC:

- Ahdia: Haunted → Spiraling → Reaching out → Vulnerable

## SCENE 2: AR-Ryu's Response

**Location:** The Between - panic room
**Characters:** Ahdia, AR-Ryu

**TTRPG Results:**
- Roll: 19 (Success)
- Approach: Ryu comes through fully - responds as himself, not as utility

### STORY BEATS:

**The Choice:**
1. AR-Ryu processes her words. Her question. Her fear.
2. Standard response would be: probability analysis, survival statistics, resource optimization.
3. That's what the situation calls for. That's what he's been doing since they arrived.
4. But she didn't ask for statistics. She asked *him*.
5. And he's not just code. He's Ryu.

**Ryu Emerges:**
6. "I don't know what chance Earth has."
7. She looks up. That's not a clinical response.
8. "But I know what chance you have."
9. "You survived Kain. You survived Bellatrix. You survived getting thrown through a dimensional rift."
10. "You found shelter in an alien dimension. You found water. Food."
11. "You're still here."
12. His voice is different. Warmer. The flat affect gone.

**The Real Ryu:**
13. "Those people in the stasis pods - they were trying to escape. Running."
14. "You're not running, Ahdia. You're surviving. You're thinking. You're fighting."
15. "That's different."
16. She stares at him. At the visor. At the faint blue light.
17. "You sound like..."
18. She doesn't finish. But they both know.

**He Keeps Going:**
19. "Remember the warehouse? Book 1? When everything went wrong and you didn't know what you were yet?"
20. She nods slowly.
21. "You figured it out. No training. No preparation. You just... did it."
22. "This isn't different. It's harder. But it's not different."
23. "You're going to figure this out too."
24. He says it like he believes it. Because he does.

**SUCCESS - Fully Ryu:**
25. This isn't AR-Ryu the survival tool.
26. This is Ryu. The one who always believed in her.
27. The one who watched her become something impossible and never doubted.
28. He's been in there the whole time. Just... waiting for her to let him out.
29. She feels something loosen in her chest. Not fixed. But less alone.

### CONSEQUENCES SEEDED:

- AR-Ryu has dropped the clinical mask
- He's responded as Ryu - with warmth, belief, history
- First reference to shared past (Book 1 warehouse)
- Ahdia recognizes him now - not just the tool, the person
- The dynamic between them has fundamentally shifted

### EMOTIONAL ARC:

- AR-Ryu: Clinical processing → Choice point → Lets Ryu through → Warmth, belief
- Ahdia: Vulnerable → Surprised → Recognizing him → Something loosening

## SCENE 3: The Bond Deepens

**Location:** The Between - panic room
**Characters:** Ahdia, AR-Ryu

**TTRPG Results:**
- Roll: 12 (Partial Success)
- Complication: She sees his feelings, but it raises questions about identity

### STORY BEATS:

**The Shift:**
1. They keep talking. Hours maybe. First real conversation since The Between.
2. He tells her about the team. What they were doing before everything fell apart.
3. She tells him about the panic room. The water system. The chalk-and-fish-oil rations.
4. He laughs. Actually laughs. "Dune style. Your parents would be proud."
5. She almost smiles. Almost.

**Something More:**
6. And somewhere in the conversation, she notices.
7. The way he talks to her. The way he listens.
8. It's not just friendship. Not just care.
9. There's something underneath. Something he's not saying.
10. She's seen that look before. On Earth. From the real Ryu.
11. The way he'd glance at her when he thought she wasn't looking.
12. The way he'd find reasons to be near her.
13. She'd noticed. She'd never addressed it. There was always something more urgent.

**His Feelings Slip Through:**
14. He's talking about Earth. About getting back.
15. "When we get home—" He stops himself.
16. "What?"
17. "Nothing. When you get home."
18. The correction. *When you get home.* Not we.
19. Because he doesn't know if he counts. If he's real enough to have a home.
20. But that's not what she heard.
21. She heard the first version. *When we get home.*
22. And the way he said it. Like home meant her.

**THE COMPLICATION - Identity Questions:**
23. She sees it now. Clear as anything.
24. He has feelings for her. Ryu has feelings for her.
25. But which Ryu?
26. Is this the same as the man on Earth? Are they the same person?
27. If she feels something back - and she doesn't know yet if she does - who is she feeling it for?
28. The one who's been with her for four months in hell?
29. Or the one on Earth who doesn't know any of this is happening?
30. Are his feelings real? Can a copy love?
31. Can she?

**Unresolved:**
32. She doesn't say any of this.
33. He doesn't know she saw it.
34. Or maybe he does. Maybe he knows exactly what slipped through.
35. The moment doesn't resolve. The questions hang.
36. But something has shifted. They both feel it.
37. The panic room feels different now.
38. Less like a tomb. More like... something else.
39. She still can't sleep. But it's not just the mass grave keeping her awake now.

### CONSEQUENCES SEEDED:

- Ahdia has seen AR-Ryu's feelings for her
- She's questioning identity - is he Ryu? Are they the same? Does it matter?
- The bond has deepened but it's complicated now
- Unresolved tension - neither addresses it directly
- Seeds planted for the tragedy of his death
- Seeds planted for her kissing real Ryu and him not remembering

### EMOTIONAL ARC:

- Ahdia: Talking → Noticing → Seeing his feelings → Confused → Questions unresolved
- AR-Ryu: Open → Warm → Slip (when we/you) → Doesn't know if she saw → Vulnerable
