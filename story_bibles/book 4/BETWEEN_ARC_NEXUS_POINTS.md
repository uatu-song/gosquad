## TTRPG APPROACH

### Starting Scenario
**Day 0**: Ahdia emerges in The Between, gateway seals behind her. AR-Ryu initializes. What does she do?

### Key Decision Points (Dice-Driven)
- Exploration vs staying put
- Risk assessment for survival needs
- Investigation of strange phenomena
- Combat/evasion when threats appear
- Resource management (visor battery, physical stamina)
- Social interaction if NPCs exist

### Success/Failure Consequences
- **Success**: Finds resources easily, avoids danger, makes progress
- **Partial success**: Achieves goal but with complications/costs
- **Failure**: Complications arise, must adapt, new problems emerge
- **Critical failure**: Serious setback, injury, resource loss

### Tracking Systems
- **Visor battery**: Drains with usage (tracking, scanning, AR functions)
- **Physical condition**: Hunger, thirst, exhaustion, injuries
- **Psychological state**: Isolation effects, stress, determination
- **Time passage**: Days in The Between (affects all above)
- **Resources**: Water, food, tools, discovered items

## RUNNING THE CAMPAIGN

1. **Start at Day 0** (Ahdia just stranded)
2. **Player declares intent** (what does Ahdia do?)
3. **Roll for outcome** (skill checks based on action)
4. **Apply consequences** (success/failure affects situation)
5. **Track resources** (battery, stamina, supplies)
6. **Introduce complications** (based on failures or random events)
7. **Repeat** until natural meeting with Suzie emerges
8. **Continue** through beacon construction and to Chapter 31 events
