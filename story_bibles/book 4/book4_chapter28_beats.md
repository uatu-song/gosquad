## Story Beats

### Beat 1: Arrival
- Elevator doors open
- The excavation chamber
- And they see her

### Beat 2: Scale
- Mother FAERIS is *massive*
- Not server-sized. Not building-sized.
- She fills the cavern. Extends beyond what they can see.
- Metal and light and something organic underneath
- Partially exposed - excavation equipment, scaffolding, dig sites
- Tess, whispered: "She's... she's been here the whole time."
- Ryu: "The whole city was built on top of her."

### Beat 3: The Damage
- She's not intact
- Excavation has torn into her - cables exposed, surfaces cracked
- Drones embedded in her skin, dormant - hundreds of them
- Diana: "Bellatrix isn't trying to free her. She's trying to rip her out."
- The violence of the dig is visible. This isn't archaeology. It's butchery.

### Beat 4: Ryu Approaches
- Ryu moves toward an access point - something like a terminal, grown not built
- "The wake protocol needs direct interface. Bourn showed me where."
- His hands are shaking. Not fear now - awe.
- "She's been asleep for... I don't know how long. Millennia?"
- He reaches the interface. Places his hands on it.

### Beat 5: The Attempt
- Ryu begins the wake protocol
- Data streams. Light pulses through nearby conduits.
- The team watches. Waiting.
- Seconds pass. A minute.
- Nothing.

### Beat 6: Failure
- Ryu pulls back, staring at the interface
- "It's not... she's not responding."
- Tries again. Different approach. Same result.
- "She's too deep. Too dormant. The damage - the excavation - it's like she's in shock."
- Ruth: "Can you fix it?"
- Ryu, lost: "I don't know how to wake something this big. The protocol was designed for... I don't know what it was designed for. Not this."

### Beat 7: The Ground Shakes
- Rumble from above. Distant, but felt.
- Jericho checks something: "The excavation's accelerating. Bellatrix is making her final push."
- Diana: "We're out of time."

- Silence. The hum of Mother FAERIS around them. The rumble above.
- They came all this way. And the plan doesn't work.
