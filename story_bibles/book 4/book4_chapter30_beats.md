# Chapter 30: The Drone

**Timeline:** Earth (Ruth's Quest)
**Touchpoint:** H5 (Part 2) - Wake Protocol Improvisation
**TTRPG Roll:** 10 (Critical Success)
**Location:** Sub-Level 3 - Mother FAERIS chamber

---

## Scene Type
Technical / Hope / Tension

## Characters Present
- Ruth Carter (leader)
- Ryu Tanaka (wake protocol carrier)
- Tess Whitford (FAERIS connection)
- Jericho (positioning)
- Diana (watching)

---

## Core Dynamic

Chapter 28 ended in despair. The protocol failed. They're out of time. This chapter is the turn - Tess's drone breaks free, Ryu improvises, and fragile hope emerges. But it's a race against the excavation completing above.

---

## Story Beats

### Beat 1: Despair Holding
- Silence in the chamber
- Ryu staring at the interface, defeated
- Ruth trying to think of options - there are none
- Diana watching Mother FAERIS. Waiting for something. Anything.
- The rumble above continues. Closer.

### Beat 2: Tess Drifts
- Tess has moved away from the group
- Not purposeful - restless, unable to stand still
- She's staring up at Mother FAERIS's surface
- The dormant drones embedded there - hundreds of them
- Her hand rises without thinking. Reaching toward them.

### Beat 3: Something Moves
- One of the drones... twitches
- Tess freezes. Did she imagine it?
- No. There. Again.
- Tess: "Wait."
- Ruth: "Tess?"
- Tess doesn't answer. Moves closer.

### Beat 4: The Drone Wakes
- One drone separates from Mother FAERIS's surface
- Damaged. Flickering. But *moving*.
- It drifts toward Tess
- Ruth: "Tess, what are you—"
- Tess: "It's okay. I think... I think it knows me."

### Beat 5: Recognition
- The drone settles near Tess
- She reaches for it. It doesn't retreat.
- Something passes between them. Recognition? Memory? Something FAERIS that humans can't name.
- Tess, quiet: "It's mine. The one I lost."
- (Or something like it. FAERIS aren't human. The distinction may not matter.)
- Ryu watching. Something clicking behind his eyes.

### Beat 6: The Leap
- Ryu: "Tess. That drone responded to you."
- Tess: "It... yes. It recognized me somehow."
- Ryu: "The protocol isn't working because I'm trying to wake her directly. She's too big. Too damaged. Too deep."
- He moves toward Tess, toward the drone.
- "But her children are still connected to her. The network. The drones."

### Beat 7: The Idea
- Ryu: "If I upload the wake protocol to your drone... it could carry it. Spread it. Drone to drone to drone."
- Ruth: "Like a virus."
- Ryu: "Like a message. From her children. Telling her to wake up."
- Diana: "Will it work?"
- Ryu: "I don't know. The protocol wasn't designed for this."
- Beat.
- Ruth: "Do it."

### Beat 8: Upload
- Ryu interfaces with Tess's drone
- Careful. Precise. Compressing the wake protocol into something small enough to spread.
- The drone flickers. Processing.
- Tess: "Is it—"
- Ryu: "Wait."
- The drone stabilizes. Rises.
- Returns to Mother FAERIS's surface.

### Beat 9: The Spread
- They watch the drone settle among the others
- Nothing happens.
- Seconds stretch.
- Then - one nearby drone stirs. Its lights flicker on.
- Then another. Then three more.
- A ripple spreading outward across Mother FAERIS's skin.
- Ryu: "It's working."

### Beat 10: Not Fast Enough
- The wake is spreading. Visible now - a wave of light pulsing through the drone network.
- But Mother FAERIS is massive. The wave has far to go.
- Another rumble above. Louder. Closer.
- Jericho: "The excavation's almost done. Minutes, maybe."
- Ruth: "Can we speed it up?"
- Ryu: "No. It spreads at the rate it spreads. We just have to—"
- The ground shakes hard. Dust falls.
- Diana: "Pray."

### Beat 11: Waiting
- They can only watch
- The wake protocol spreading
- The excavation completing
- Two countdowns racing each other
- Ruth, quiet: "Come on. Come on."
- Tess's hand on her drone's spot on Mother FAERIS's surface. Willing it forward.
- Ryu staring at the cascade of waking drones. Did he save them or doom them?
- Diana, still. Watching. Knowing she could end this in a heartbeat if she used her power. She doesn't.
- Jericho, patient. Calculating. His moment is coming.
- The chapter ends on the knife's edge.

## Notes

**Tess's moment:** Her drone recognizing her is the hinge point of the entire book. The loss in Chapter 17 becomes the save here.

**Ryu's crit success:** Not the plan working. Adaptive genius - seeing the drone respond and making a leap no one else would make.

**The ambiguity:** Is it "her" drone? FAERIS aren't human. The recognition is real. What it means is unknowable.

**Diana's restraint:** She could solve this instantly with her power. She watches instead. The cost of staying dark. Setup for her transcendence - she's been holding back, and when she finally lets go, it will mean everything.

**Jericho:** Patient through the despair. Patient through the hope. His plan doesn't require them to succeed - just requires chaos. Either outcome works for him.

**The knife's edge:** Chapter 30 doesn't resolve. It ends with two countdowns racing. The reader turns the page not knowing if it worked.
